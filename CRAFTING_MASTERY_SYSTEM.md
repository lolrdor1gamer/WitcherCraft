# WitcherCraft - Crafting Mastery & Profession System

## Overview
A comprehensive crafting progression system where players specialize in professions, gain mastery levels, unlock advanced techniques, and create signature items. This transforms crafting from simple recipes into a deep skill-based experience with meaningful choices.

**Signature Item Process:**
```
1. Reach Grandmaster (Lvl 75+) in a profession
2. Obtain rare "Signature Crafting Token" (quest reward)
3. Choose base item to elevate (any legendary you crafted)
4. Enter Signature Crafting UI:
   - Name your item (custom name)
   - Choose unique effect from list (profession-specific)
   - Write custom lore (100 character limit)
5. Craft using special materials:
   - Base item
   - 10x Rare Profession Materials
   - 1x Essence of Mastery (obtained from mastery quest)
   - 1000 Gold (crafting fee)
6. Item becomes Signature (Gold tier, soulbound)
```

**Unique Effects by Profession:**

**Blacksmith (Weapons):**
- Bloodthirst: Gain +5% damage per kill (stacks to 50%)
- Unbreakable: Item never loses durability
- Lifesteal: 10% damage healed as HP
- Chain Lightning: 20% chance to strike 3 nearby enemies
- Execute: Deal 3x damage to enemies below 20% HP

**Blacksmith (Armor):**
- Ironclad: Take 50% less damage when below 30% HP
- Regeneration: +2 HP/sec passive heal
- Reflect: 15% melee damage reflected to attacker
- Elemental Immunity: Immune to one element (fire/ice/poison)
- Second Wind: Automatic revive at 1 HP (once per day)

**Alchemy (Potions):**
- Infinite Brew: Potion has unlimited uses (never consumed)
- Enhanced Absorption: 2x potion effect strength
- No Toxicity: Potion generates zero toxicity
- Shared Blessing: Nearby allies gain 50% of potion effect
- Instant Effect: Potion works immediately (no delay)

**Alchemy (Oils):**
- Eternal Oil: Never depletes (permanent coating)
- Multi-Target: Oil works on ALL monster types
- Explosive Strikes: 25% chance for AOE explosion on hit
- Corrosive: Reduces enemy armor by 20% permanently
- Vampiric: Killing coated enemy restores full HP

**Runecrafting (Enchantments):**
- Triple Stack: Rune effect applies 3 times
- Adaptive: Rune changes element based on enemy weakness
- Mana Burst: Kills restore 25% Sign Intensity
- Soul Trap: Killing enemy captures soul (crafting material)
- Resonance: All runes on item gain +50% effectiveness

```

- 2 crafting stations
- +10% craft speed
- Can store 108 materials

- 3 crafting stations
- +25% craft speed
- Can store 216 materials
- 2 Apprentice NPCs

TIER 4: GRAND WORKSHOP (500,000 gold)
- All 5 crafting stations
- +50% craft speed (stacks with mastery)
- Infinite material storage (auto-sort)
- 5 Apprentice NPCs (can assign tasks)
```

**Workshop Benefits:**

```
- Apprentices: Gain XP when they craft
```

## ğŸ¯ Profession Quests

### **Mastery Quest Chain**
Each profession has a questline unlocking at Journeyman (Lvl 25), Master (Lvl 50), and Grandmaster (Lvl 75).

**Example: Blacksmithing Questline**

**Journeyman Quest (Level 25): "Trial of Iron"**
```
Objective: Prove your skill to a Master Blacksmith

1. Craft a Flawless Steel Sword (0 mistakes, 1 attempt)
2. Repair 10 damaged weapons for townsfolk
3. Smelt 50 iron ore perfectly (no waste)
4. Defeat 5 bandits using a weapon YOU crafted

Reward:
- Specialization unlock (choose path)
- +500 Blacksmithing XP
- Recipe: "Temerian Steel Sword" (enhanced)
- Title: "Journeyman Blacksmith"
```

**Master Quest (Level 50): "Forging Legends"**
```
Objective: Craft a legendary blade for a noble

1. Obtain Meteorite Ore (rare material from meteor crash site)
2. Gather 10 Witcher's Silver (trade with Witchers)
3. Find Ancient Blacksmithing Tome (dungeon loot)
4. Craft "Aerondight" sword (unique recipe)
5. Temper blade in Dragon's Breath (boss fight required)

Reward:
- Master Rank (Masterwork crafting unlocked)
- +2000 Blacksmithing XP
- 10x Greater Runestones
- Recipe: "Mastercrafted Witcher Gear" (all sets)
- Title: "Master Blacksmith"
```

**Grandmaster Quest (Level 75): "Forge of Legends"**
```
Objective: Reforge a legendary artifact

1. Find shattered pieces of "Gvalch'ca" (5 locations)
2. Convince Elder Smith to teach secret technique (reputation)
3. Gather Essence of Fire (elemental plane boss)
4. Reforge blade at ancient volcanic forge (Skellige)
5. Prove blade's worth (slay legendary monster)

Reward:
- Grandmaster Rank (Legendary crafting unlocked)
- +5000 Blacksmithing XP
- Signature Crafting Token (1x, for signature item)
- Essence of Mastery (signature item material)
- Recipe: "Gvalch'ca" (legendary sword)
- Title: "Grandmaster Blacksmith"
- Cosmetic: "Forge Master's Apron" (outfit)
```
